Fee information


The child is at the heart of all that we do and we are committed to ensuring that they can achieve their full potential, thus we have 3 purposeful and stimulating rooms containing age appropriate, multi-sensory and challenging resources.

Our Mini Monkey room caters for babies and toddlers from 3 months to 2 years old, the Little Lions room is for 2-3 year olds, and the Jolly Giraffes room is for children from 3-5 years. For more information on the rooms at Nellie's, please go to the Nursery Rooms section of our website.

Our care and education is based on the Early Years Foundation Stage framework.

Milk, Meals and Snacks



If your baby or toddler is still having milk, we ask that you supply this and we can keep it refrigerated as required.

For babies and toddlers under 14 months, we offer a package whereby you supply your child's meals and snacks for the day or session.

Alternatively, for babies and toddlers under 14 months, and for all toddlers from 14 months, we offer our standard package where meals and snacks will be provided within the session rates. All meals and snacks will be prepared on-site, providing a nutritious and varied diet, perfectly balanced for the growing nutritional needs of this age group.

​All meals and snacks will be prepared on-site, providing a nutritious diet perfectly balanced for this age. Allergies and special dietary requirements will be catered for, just bring it to the attention of a member of staff.

Mini Monkeys



Our Mini Monkeys rooms accommodates babies and toddlers from 3 months to 2 years old. We work closely with parents to deliver the best individual care for each child, developing a routine based on their own needs and stage of development. This ensures that their feeding and sleeping times fit with their own natural rhythms.



Areas

Calm & Cot Area



Situated in a separate room, this room is a cosy, comfortable and relaxing space ideal for sleep or rest. Babies and toddlers can sleep in the designated cot area which can be screened off, on a sleeping mat or however they find most relaxing. When not used for sleep, this area is ideal for a cosy milk feed or a quiet story.

Messy Play Area


Situated in the main room, babies and toddlers can explore a wide range of messy play using all of their senses. A low level sink is provided in this area to encourage toddlers to begin to develop their independence and wash their own hands.

Physical Play Area



Even when it's just too wet for the babies and toddlers to get outside and develop their gross motor skills, our spacious room gives them lots of space to develop these key skills.
