The Courtyard

A flat area hard-standing area, the courtyard is richly resourced with play materials to encourage all areas of learning and development. We will have a construction site containing various building equipment, a small world and role play area to encourage imaginative play, a music and movement area to promote creativity, sand and water trays to explore, and plenty of mark making opportunities. We will also have a wide variety of ride-ons and other toys to promote physical development.

The Mini Monkey room has a fenced off area in the courtyard which they can access at all times during the day. They will also have planned access to the rest of the courtyard and the nature garden.



The Nature Gatden

An inspiring natural area, the nature garden which will allow children to investigate the open-ended natural resources to develop and test their ideas and understanding about the world they live in. We will have a sensory garden which will be a relaxing and reflective space, a mud kitchen, a small world area, a digging area with a variety of tools and equipment, and a natural music area containing stick xylophones and a music wall made of pots and pans. We will also have equipment to encourage den building and minibeast hunts, and a fantastic vegetable patch which the children will decide what they would like to grow and learn how to look after.
